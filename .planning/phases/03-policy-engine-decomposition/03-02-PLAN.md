# Phase 3 Plan 02: Introduce side-effect-free decision engine and action executor

## Objective
- **What:** Separate policy decision evaluation from side-effect execution using structured internal interfaces.

- **Output:** `DecisionResult`, `PolicyEngine`, and `ActionExecutor` integrated into redirector flow.
